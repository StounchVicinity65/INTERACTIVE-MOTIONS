Why Pixels Instead of Meters?

We use pixels because your computer screen is made of them. Simulations run on screen space‚Äînot real-world meters. Measuring in pixels makes drawing, collision, and animation easier to handle inside a canvas.

Why Frame Instead of Seconds?

We use frames because animations are updated frame-by-frame, usually at 60 frames per second. Rather than syncing with real time, this keeps motion smooth and predictable regardless of FPS.
