Why the Scale Factor (‡¶∏‡ßç‡¶ï‡ßá‡¶≤ ‡¶´‡ßç‡¶Ø‡¶æ‡¶ï‡ßç‡¶ü‡¶∞ ‡¶ï‡ßá‡¶®)?

Most screens use pixels to display content, and the physical size of a pixel can vary significantly between devices. Since we‚Äôre working within a computer screen for visual representation, measuring positions and distances in **pixels** is logical. To connect these screen pixels to real-world SI units (like meters), a specific scale factor is applied.
